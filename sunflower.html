Sunflower

Before

I start off all my shoe designs by buying the shoes from a thrift store. I purchased these for a discounted rate online.


Sketch + Ideation

I knew I wanted sunflowers on them when I started the design, but I wasn't sure how exactly they would take up the shoe. I decided they would be on the side but I needed to practice my form. I drew on the sides of the shoe with pencil first and then followed with acrylic paint.


Final Product

This is my final product. While painting the shoes I realized I needed to outline the shape of the flower to make the design stand out more. This is my first customization.
